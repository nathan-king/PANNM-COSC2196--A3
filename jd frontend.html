Front End Developer

Position: Front End Developer
Department: IT
Position: Project Manager
Job Summary:
We are looking for a qualified Front End Developer to join our team. You will be responsible for building and implementing the visual elements that clients and users see and interact with (TalentLyft 2021).
You will be required to work in a team of four supervised by the project manager.
You should be able to create a practical and user-friendly application that represents our company.
This position will provide you with valuable experience to assist in the advancement of your career.
As we are a startup company, this will be a six-month contracted position, with ongoing maintenance work when required.
Please note: a contract and non-disclosure agreement will be required to ensure intellectual property is protected, and remains the property of PANNM.
Responsibilties and Duties:
• Using a variety of markup languages to create user-friendly web pages and apps.
• Work with a team to build a web page and app.
• Maintain and improve the website and app.
• Assist with/ build on prototypes.
• Provide high-quality graphics.
• Build reusable code.
• Optimise applications for maximum speed.
• Maintain brand consistency.
• Build on design and come up with solutions based on user and customer feedback.
• Keep up to date with the latest technologies, attending training where required.
Key Relationships:
Internal • Project Manager
• Back End Developer
• Graphics Designer/ User Interface Builder
• Marketing Manager
Key Decision Making in this Role:
• Determining structure and design.
Key Chanllenges:
• Managing client expectations and educating the client.
• Ensuring the product functions consistently across all devices.
• Balancing automated testing.
• Debugging JavaScript code.
• Keeping the site/app easy to maintain.
(Jakob 2015)
Qualifications and Experience:
Qualifications: • Bachelor of Computer Science,
• Bachelor of Information Technology, or
• similar qualification
Experience: • Experience as a Front End Developer or prior work experience.
• Experience with CSS, HTML, JavaScript, and JQuery
Knowledge/skills: • Understanding of web and app development process.
• Understanding JavaScript Framework, such as React Native.
• Understanding of version control systems such as Git.
• Knowledge of browser testing and debugging.
• Knowledge of content management systems (CMS)
• Knowledge of graphics software such as Adobe Suite and photoshop.
• Knowledge of SEO principles.
• Knowledge of markup languages.
• Strong problem-solving skills.
• Strong interpersonal and communication skills.
• Strong time-management skills.
Personal Requirements: • Ability to work independently when required.
• Ability to work in a team.
• Strong work ethic and attention to detail.
• Sense of ownership and pride.
