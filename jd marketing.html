Marketing Manager

Position: Marketing Manager
Department: IT
Position: Project Manager
Job Summary:
Our Marketing Manager’s primary responsibility will be overseeing the entire marketing aspect of the product. The Marketing Manager will work with advertising agencies to run appropriate marketing campaigns.
This will be done by providing ideas on ensuring a positive relationship between the brand and clients, finding ways of optimizing demand for the product and analyzing customer feedback from social media and other platforms.
This role aims to ensure PANNM has consistent growth over time and the ability to attract potential customers that fall both inside and outside our target audience.
As we are a startup company, this will be a six-month contracted position, with ongoing maintenance work when required.
Please note: a contract and non-disclosure agreement will be required to ensure intellectual property is protected, and remains the property of PANNM.
Responsibilties and Duties:
The Key duties and responsibilities for this role include, but are not limited to:
• Setting the marketing strategy for the app.
• Researching and analyzing market trends and competitors.
• Overseeing marketing campaigns.
• Monitoring marketing campaign success.
• Maintaining budget of marketing department and ensuring investment presents returns.
• Managing the design of promotional materials.
Key Relationships:
Internal • Project Manager
• Front End Developer
• Back End Developer
• Graphics Designer/ User Interface Builder
Key Decision Making in this Role:
• Deciding on the most effective way to spend the marketing budget.
• Decisions on pricing strategy.
• Decisions on the most effective marketing strategy and campaigns.
Key Chanllenges:
• Working within a budget
• Finding opportunities to optimize product demand.
• Research of customer needs.
(Marketing91, 2019)
Qualifications and Experience:
Qualifications: • Diploma of Marketing and Communication (Or higher qualification).
Experience: • Experience in a similar marketing role.
Knowledge/skills: • Interpersonal and communication skills.
• Strong analytical skills.
• Critical thinking.
• Networking ability.
• Good organization and planning skills.
• Creativity.
Personal Requirements: • Ability to work in a team.
• Strong work ethic and attention to detail.
• Ability to meet tight deadlines.
