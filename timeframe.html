Time Frame | Task List

While a full task list for PANNM’s project USE-ME NOW – Use By Date Tracker Application is below, the immediate tasks competed by PANNM team members over the initial weeks of the project are outlined below. These weeks include the beginning of January, when the original ideas were conceived as part of Assessment 2.

Week 1 | Commencing 04th January 2021 | Draft Concept and proposed work requirements.

The first week of the project. The team came together to form PANNM and worked through the project's original concept ideas, drafting a skeleton of the work requirements and distributing workloads. Nathan set up the team PANNM repository on GitHub, and the remainder of the team were designated sections to start work.

Week 2 | Commencing 11th January 2021 | research and review of project and website commencement.

The team continued to complete all written work and set up each of the blank pages for the website to be developed. The team spent time honing the newly acquired web site building skills with the assistance of Nathan and Alasdair coaching Phil, Michelle and Nick.

Michelle took ill this week, though pushed on. The team reviewed the project after further research revealed similar products on the market, with four of them available in Australia. Team PANNM worked to differentiate our idea from the others in the market place and elected to proceed with the project.

The team distributed the workload to complete the written report by Sunday evening.

Week 3 | Commencing 18th January 2021 | Submission of first written report and Website.

With a written report completed, website workloads were distributed this week, with each team member initially allocated sections of the website. Regular touchpoints throughout the week ensured as each section of the website was finalised, the team moved straight on to the next area.
